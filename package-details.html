Itinerary

day 1

Arrival to Cairo Airport , having the service of MEET & ASSIST ,then transfer to the Hotel spending there 4 nights with breakfast ,over night at the hotel

day 2

Breakfast at the hotel, then moving to see Sakkara which is having the step pyramid, the oldest pyramid of Egypt, then having lunch in a typical restaurant, after that moving to Pyramids of Giza, one of the seven wonders all over the world, then over night at the hotel

day 3

Breakfast at the hotel then moving to see Egyptian museum with its wonder pieces of art ,then going to see the citadel of Saladin with the alabaster mosque of Mohamed Ali ,then having lunch in a typical restaurant ,then moving to see Old Cairo to visit the Coptic churches and the synagogue ,then back to the hotel ,over night at the hotel

day 4

Free day if you want to visit Alexandria as an OPTIONAL VISIT and come back at the same day

day 5

Breakfast at the hotel, then check out, transfer to Cairo airport for having the departure flight
